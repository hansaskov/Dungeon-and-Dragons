## Diary
When the expedition party [[Holy order]] met up with [[Harold Jamish (deceased)]] carrying a object wrapped in a white piece of cloth. The object where around the size of **Harlod** himself, and even when covered, a comfortable warm aura was emanating from it. It was given over to our team, where our leader, a paladin named [[Zyroth Steelclaw]] took charge of its protection. He gave no notion of what the artifacts powers were. When he prodded further [[Zyroth Steelclaw]] became very defensive and almost hostile. Though through the travels he did hear him whisper to the artifact multiple times a day, sadly I could never really hear what he said. When we got to sea, the artifact was locked into a room and no one could access it. The first few days went without problem, but one night we suddenly came under fire. A group who called themselves **Liberators** boarded the ship. They started talking about saving us from the our blinded faith, and how by following them, they would show us the true god and savoir of **Lycra**. Negotiations broke down, and fighting started. I... Bravely secured the food storage for the fight, ensuring no one came in. We won the battle but the prisoners all died via suicide or something before we could interrogate them. No other attack happened while at sea, and when we got to **Lycra** me and the other merchant guys left ahead of the paladin guards. They stated that they needed to secure a more safe transport now that they were attacked. My guess is that they are calling in the [[Fang Guard]].
