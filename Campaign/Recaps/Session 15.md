## Entering the sewers
The party upon entering the sewers together with the two other rookie adventure parties are met with a dim lit, open polygon room. Small indents in the ground pave a road for wagons of waste to be carried up and out of the sewers the same way they entered, while in the centre of the room a large pit filled with waste could be found. 
The party spots a group of workers next to the pit, pulling op metal wagons filled with waste and empty them into wagons, which are sealed by another group of workers. They choose to talk with one of the workers and enquire about the sewers some more. They are told that their job now have become harder to maintain, since no-one can enter into the deeper parts of the sewers safely. 

While talking they notice that the other parties have ventured into the sewers, not knowing which way they went, they decide to go south.

## Mapping and first dangers

While venturing into the sewers, the party mapped their travels so that they could get a idea of their surroundings and have a lactation if they found something related to **the umbral court**. While exploring they encounter multiple natural dangers such as yellow and brown mold, as well as some of the monsters they were told to hunt.

One encounter with a otyugh within it's lair, nearly killed [[Borgemir Plainsrunner]], but due to the healing from [[Caelum Aequoris]] and expert precision of [[Luffy Longfinger]]  killing it in the end, they all came out with all limbs intact.

The session ended when the party entered a large room housing a waste pit and pumps, as well as a black and yellow slime.
